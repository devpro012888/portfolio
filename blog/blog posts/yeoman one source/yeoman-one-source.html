Introducing Yeoman One Source: An All-in-One Tool Built for Coast Guard Yeoman

The all-in-one resource for Coast Guard Yeoman

February 23, 2026

From the Deckplates to the Development Environment

If you've ever served as a United States Coast Guard Yeoman, you know the drill — dozens of browser bookmarks, scattered manuals, multiple websites open at once, and still somehow not finding the answer you need fast enough. Pay and Personnel Center (PPC), ETS, DTOD, DTMO — the list goes on. That frustration is exactly what inspired my first major software project: Yeoman One Source.

My background is rooted in the Coast Guard Yeoman rating. I spent years assisting members with pay, personnel, and travel matters — and I experienced firsthand how fragmented and time-consuming it can be to find accurate information quickly. Now, as a Software Engineer, I'm channeling that experience into a tool built specifically for the YN community.

What is Yeoman One Source?

Yeoman One Source is a centralized web application designed to be the single destination for everything a Coast Guard Yeoman needs to do their job. The goal is simple: reduce the time spent hunting across multiple websites and manuals by bringing it all together in one place.

The site is organized into four core sections, each derived from Rating Performance Qualifications:

  • Administration
  • Pay and Personnel
  • Reserves
  • Travel and Transportation

Each section features a dropdown menu covering a comprehensive range of relevant topics. As development progresses, some topics may be consolidated to keep navigation clean and intuitive.

Key Features

Commonly Used Links: The homepage will feature quick-access links to the tools Yeomen use every day — Direct Access, ETS, iPERMS, MLINQS, DPS, and more — eliminating the need to maintain an overwhelming bookmark collection.

Yeoman Resource Hub: A dedicated section will provide access to Calculators, Forms and Worksheets, References, and additional resources, all organized and easy to find.

Smart Search: A homepage search bar will allow Yeomen to query any topic and receive instant results — no more digging through PDFs or navigating outdated websites. This feature alone has the potential to save significant time on a daily basis.

Order Notes Generator: One of the most anticipated features, the Order Notes Generator will allow Yeomen to enter member information into a structured form and automatically generate formatted order notes — ready to copy and paste directly into Direct Access. What once took careful manual drafting becomes a streamlined, error-reduced workflow.

Date and Travel Calculators: Service date computations and travel calculations will be built into the platform. Yeomen will be able to input dates or travel details and receive accurate outputs instantly — no more pencil-and-paper math or cross-referencing spreadsheets. The Travel Calculation Sheet, in particular, will automatically compute a member's travel entitlements based on their trip details.

Yeoman GPT (Coming Soon): Perhaps the most ambitious feature on the roadmap is Yeoman GPT — an AI-powered chatbot trained on Yeoman references and manuals. Any Coast Guard member will be able to ask a Yeoman-related question and receive an accurate, sourced answer in seconds. Think of it as having a knowledgeable Yeoman available around the clock. This feature will require extensive testing and refinement, and is planned as one of the final additions to the platform.

Current Development Status

The homepage is currently in active development. From there, work will begin on the individual pages for each of the four main subject areas. Once the core build is complete, the platform will enter a structured testing phase involving additional Coast Guard members to validate accuracy, usability, and reliability before any public release.
